## Overview of the Planets
The planets of our solar system are divided into two main groups: inner terrestrial planets
(Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars) and outer gas and ice giants (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune).
Each planet has unique characteristics, ranging from extreme temperatures to massive storms
and complex ring systems.
